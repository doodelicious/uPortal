Customizing myBYU

myBYU provides a simple approach for navigation and personalization. With myBYU you can:
  • Reorder the portal content layout by dragging and dropping to another location on the page
  • Search or browse for portal content ("Add Content")
  • Add, remove, rename, and change the order of tabs ("Page Layout")
  • Modifying the number of columns for each tab ("Page Layout")
  • Select a different skin ("Select Skin")
  • Configure some portal content to meet your needs
  • Easily navigate to key services on campus using multiple portlets: Bookmarks, University Favorites, Quicklinks, RouteY Links
